Bot's Summary PEEKY is a fancy Discord bot that comes with a list of functions, a rich profile system, moderation commands and a music player!

Functions

PEEKY stores alot of great functions that range from good Streaming Utilities to some of the most responsible Anti-Raid techniques on Discord.
Profile System

PEEKY has a Profile System with customizable backgrounds, a global leaderboard, descriptions, badges, chests, levels and alot more!
Moderation Commands

PEEKY has a selection of good and unique moderation commands for you and your server moderators!
Music Player

PEEKY has a simple but solid music player that alllows you and your Discord friends to listen to songs together!
Minigames (WIP)

PEEKY can provide a bunch fun minigames to ensure a pleasent time for you and your friends!
