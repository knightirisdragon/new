Badges These are all the badges that you can earn for your profile.
Keep in mind that some badges require you to have a role in the Support Server.

Staff
Exclusive badge for the staff in the Support Server.

- Be a staff member in the Support Server.

+ 2 Exp Gain

Premium
Awarded to PEEKY's Premium users.

- Purchase the Premium status in the store every month.

+ 2 Gredit Gain
+ 2 Exp Gain

Booster
Awarded to the current server boosters in the Support Server.

- Boost the Support Server every month.

+ 2 Gredit Gain
+ 2 Exp Gain

Translator
Awarded to people that help translate PEEKY into more languages.

- Translate PEEKY using the provided Translation Sheet.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Veteran
Awarded to the people that were in the Support Server for a long time.

- Be in the Support Server for over a year.

+ 2 Exp Gain

Upgrader
Awarded to the people that got a server upgraded.

- Upgrade an arbitrary Discord server.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Contributor
Awarded to the people that have acquired any store items, excluding merch.

- Purchase any item that's displayed in the store.
- Win any store item from giveaways or events.

+ 2 Exp Gain

Bug Hunter
Awarded to the people that help keeping PEEKY stable.

- Report a bug to PEEKY's developers and get it fixed.

+ 2 Exp Gain

Celebrator
Awarded to the people that have participated in either PEEKY's Birthday or Christmas events atleast once.

- Paricipate in a PEEKY's Birthday event.
- Paricipate in a Christmas event.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Ambassador
Awarded to the people that have participated in the Ambassador Program.

- Participate in the Ambassador Program by inviting your friends to the Support Server.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Movie Nighter
Awarded to the people that have participated in one of the Movie Night events.

- Paricipate in a Movie Night event.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Medallist
Awarded to the people that have one of the highest PEEKY Profiles.

- Reach and stay in the TOP 3 position in the leaderboard of any type.

+ 2 Exp Gain
Evil
Awarded to the people that have low enough karma.

- Get 50 negative karma.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Good
Awarded to the people that have high enough karma.

- Get 50 positive karma.

+ 2 Exp Gain

Virus
Awarded to people that have been pinged by someone that already had this badge.

- No longer obtainable as the spreading was disabled.

+ 2 Exp Gain

Party
Awarded to the people that listen to PEEKY's music player with their friends.

- Finish a song with 5 total users in a voice chat channel.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Receiver
Awarded to the people that have participated in the Ambassador Program.

- Participate in the Ambassador Program by using your friend's invite link.

+ 2 Exp Gain

Poller
Awarded to the people that participate in PEEKY's development.

- Vote on the newest polls in the Support Server.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Voter
Awarded to the people that voted for PEEKY.

- No longer obtainable as the Daily command was removed.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Completionist
Awarded to the people that like to complete challenges.

- Complete one of the current challenges.

+ 2 Gredit Gain

Publisher
Awarded to the people that contribute to PEEKY's background gallery.

- Submit a background for PEEKY in the Support Server.

+ 2 Exp Gain

Ownership
Awarded to the people that have tried out PEEKY.

- Add PEEKY to your Discord server.

+ 1 Exp Gain

Functional
Awarded to the people that use PEEKY's functions.

- Toggle a function's state.
- Set a new setting for a function.

+ 1 Exp Gain

Gopbot

- Add both PEEKY and GOPBOT to your Discord server.

Miner

Gamer

Gambler
