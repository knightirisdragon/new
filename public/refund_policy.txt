REFUND POLICY
Last updated: 25th of May, 2019

Refunds will be issued in most cases.
Bully refund requests ("Refund me or else...") will not be accepted.

How to issue a refund:
- Find the Twitter of PEEKY's current owner in the Contacts tab of the home page.
- Start a private conversation on Twitter and ask for refund by stating your situation.
- We'll go on from there.

Reasons for a potentional refund:
 - Fraud or Unauthorized Payments, most likely will require a proof of some kind.
 - Not receiving the rewards after getting the confirmation message.
 - Accidental gifting, may not be accepted on repeated occasions.
 
 If your refund was accepted:
 - You will receive your money as soon as possible.
 - Your purchased item(s) and all the purchase bonuses will be removed from your or a gifted user's profile.
 
To issue a refund, reach out in PEEKY's owner Twitter DMs.
