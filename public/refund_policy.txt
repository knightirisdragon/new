REFUND POLICY
Last updated: 21st of April, 2019

Refunds will be issued in most cases.
Bully refund requests ("Refund me or else...") will not be accepted.

Reasons for a potentional refund:
 - Fraud or Unauthorized Payments, most likely will require a proof of some kind.
 - Issues with Redeeming like not receiving the item.
 - Accidental gifting, may not be accepted on repeated occasions.
 
 If your refund was accepted:
 - You will receive your money as soon as possible.
 - Your purchased item(s) and all the bonuses will be removed from your profile.
 
To issue a refund, reach out in PEEKY's owner Twitter DMs.
